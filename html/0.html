Beautiful 5 bedroom 4 bath home in the heart of Highlands Ranch ready to move in. This two story home with a fully finished basement offers tall vaulted ceilings, large windows, high-end finishes, oversized kitchen island, double oven, stainless steel appliances, and wooden blinds. The endless mountain views from the master bedroom will make you want to get up and hike the miles of trails that are just 5 minutes walking distance along with parks, restaurants and shopping. The patio features a beautiful stamped concrete floor surrounded by fruit trees and mature vegetation. Don't miss the incredible basement with a dance studio, bar area, and theater room. Extremely near great schools K-12! Pick where you work out with four rec centers with indoor/outdoor pools, weight rooms, basketball, tennis, and indoor soccer courts, batting cages, and more. * The 5th bedroom can be either the office or the dance studio. .. or easily convert both and have 6 bedrooms!
